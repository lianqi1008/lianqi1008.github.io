I am a Ph.D. student in Information and Communication Engineering at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/), advised by Prof. Guo Lu and Prof. Wenjun Zhang. Before that, I received my M.S. degree from Beijing Jiaotong University and my B.S. degree from Wuhan University of Technology.

My research interests include vision-language models, agents, trustworthy reasoning, multimodal content moderation, and efficient visual representations. I am especially interested in building reliable multimodal systems that can reason over safety policies, compress visual tokens, and operate efficiently in real-world settings.

I have worked on trustworthy multimodal moderation, controllable generative image compression, token pruning for efficient VLMs, and long-video token compression. My recent work includes **Hi-Guard** for policy-aligned multimodal moderation and **Control-GIC** for once-for-all controllable generative image compression.
