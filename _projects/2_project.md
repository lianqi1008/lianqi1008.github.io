Control-GIC is a controllable generative image compression framework that trains once and supports arbitrary compression-rate inference.

The method uses dynamic granularity adaptation and variable-length discrete tokens to control bitrates continuously while preserving high-information image regions at low rates.

- Paper: [arXiv:2406.00758](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.00758)
- Code: [lianqi1008/Control-GIC](https://github.com/lianqi1008/Control-GIC)
